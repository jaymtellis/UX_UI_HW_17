About

With medical devices and their interfaces, design is the intermediary between the esoteric processes and experience patient/user input. I love discovering the root of problems that wasn’t known and truly emphasizing with the end user to design something that is useful and effective. Biomedical engineering experience helped me think critically about user needs and how to translate insights into product requirements, then design inputs. How to test those inputs to ensure it meets those user needs. With over 4 years of experience of designing and developing products, I look forward to bridging the gap of healthcare and technology with patients being the center focus..

Skills

User Research

Capability to empathize with user’s needs to discover insights, ideate solutions and verify those solutions through low/hi-fidelity prototype testing.

Visual Design

User Interaction
